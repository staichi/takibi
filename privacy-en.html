Takibi Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Takibi ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects the following information:

  • Email address (when creating an account)
  • Campsite, gear, and event data you register
  • Photos you upload
  • Anonymous device information for ad delivery (Google AdMob)
  • Location data (when registering campsites)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the App's services
  • For user authentication
  • To deliver advertisements (Google AdMob)
  • To recommend products through Amazon Associates

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Data storage and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdMob: Ad delivery (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Sign-In: Social login
  • Apple Sign-In: Social login
  • Amazon Associates: Product links and affiliate program

4. Data Storage and Management

Your data is securely stored on Supabase servers. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

5. Advertising

The App displays ads via Google AdMob. You can remove ads by upgrading to the Premium plan. Ad personalization can be adjusted through Google's privacy settings.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes within the App.
